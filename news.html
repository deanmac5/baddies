Registrations for Maddies Netball Club 2018

Registrations for Maddies Netball Club for season 2018 are open from the 2nd Jan 2018.

For all registration information click on the "Registration" button above.

Dont forget if you are playing with a school team in 2018 you must register with the school first - if you would like to play a second game with Maddies please register your interest by emailing maddies@maddiesnc.net

Also please register early to enable the committee plenty of time to plan the 2018 season.
