2019 Winter Competition

Below are the instructions for the Maddies Netball Club Selection Trials for 2019

If you haven't registered yet please do so as soon as possible – although you don’t have to be registered to trial with us, so please still come along All trials will be at TNA courts commencing on Wednesday 13 Feb and go for unto 3 weeks if required You will need to sign on and get a number before you trial so look for the sign on station when you arrive – please be early to help speed up the process

Juniors

Sign on will commence at 4.30. Trials will begin at 5pm sharp and go to 6.00 Note if you are playing in a grade 6 Maddies team and wish to play a second game in Inters you will also need to register with the inters group as well before the Inters trials – there will be a separate sign on station for Inters.

Inters

Sign on will commence at around 5.30. Trials will go from 6.15 until 7.15 Note if you want to play a second game in Seniors you will also need to register with the seniors group as well before the seniors trial – there will be a separate sign on station for seniors.

Seniors

Sign on will commence at around 7pm and trials will go from 7.30 until 8.30 depending on numbers.

Volunteers

Dave will be looking for volunteers to man the sign on stations for each age group. If anyone would like to help out and can be at the courts early please let him know.
