Net Set Go!

Would you like your child to learn the skills required to play netball in a fun and safe environment? Would you like your child to make friends whilst getting fit and healthy? ENROL YOUR CHILD INTO NetSetGo at Tuggeranong Netball Association. The program is open for children aged 4-9years of age. TNA will be running a Saturday Session 9am-10am each week on the dates below. TNA are looking into the possibility of an afternoon during the week but this is yet to be confirmed. The cost for the 10-week program is $95, payment is online when you register.
