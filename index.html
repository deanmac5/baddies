Welcome

Welcome to the Maddies Netball Club, one of the largest clubs within the Tuggeranong Netball Association (TNA). At Maddies we offer the opportunity for girls and women of all ages to participate in a safe, friendly and competitive sporting environment.

Our aim is to offer individual, team and social skill improvements to all players no matter what their skill level. We encourage families to be involved and we at Maddies see ourselves as a family orientated club. In addition, you will not miss our teams as our uniform is Cornflower Blue and Hot Pink.

If you are looking for a friendly, competitive netball club in the Tuggeranong area, drop us a line - we would love to hear from you. We have great sponsors, lots of social functions (BBQ's, parties etc) and encourage everyone to join in. We have a rich history having been formed in 1991 and the club going from strength to strength to now being seen as one of the most successful clubs within TNA. In addition to our team successes over many years our club has also been able to have individual success with many players representing the association in both junior and senior representative teams on a yearly basis and also some players being selected to play for the ACT in national squads.
